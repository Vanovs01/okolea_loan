Frequently Asked Questions

What types of loans do you offer?

We offer a variety of loan solutions tailored to your needs, including personal loans, business loans, school fees loans, hospital loans, car loans, and land acquisition loans.

What are the eligibility requirements?

To be eligible for a loan, you must be a Kenyan citizen, at least 18 years old, have a valid M-Pesa account, and meet other criteria as determined by Okolea Mwananchi.

How do I apply for a loan?

Applying for a loan is easy! Simply visit our website and click on the "Apply Now" button. Fill out the application form with your details and submit it. We will review your application and get back to you shortly.
